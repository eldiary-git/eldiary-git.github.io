    Passenger- Centric Experience Design in Smart Mobility by DM TECH

    Publication

    Dubai World Congress for Self-Driving Transport- RTA, October 2019, Dubai, UAE.

    DM TECH present their research on human- centered design for smart mobility experience during the self- driving transport congress held by Dubai RTA The research focused on extracting a general experience anatomy layout that matches smart mobility specifications and expectations. The research results were represented in a design procedure that allows a selective and customized vehicle/ station elements to be included/ excluded based on the focus passenger- centric needs. Also, the research discusses an optimization procedure using multiple system/ context/ human influence factors for current or futuristic transportation systems.

    Educational Program

    Designing & Building the First Hyperloop Capsule in MEA

    Team Advisors

    SpaceX Hypperloop Competition, August 2017, LA- California, USA.

    DMTECH founders and partners supervise the SpaceX Hyperloop competition team “HyperNova” that won SpaceX Innovation Award in 2016. The team design for the Hyperloop capsule was selected as a finalist from more than a thousand team submissions to show and test their Hyperloop pod in SpaceX Hyperloop track in LA, California. The team with DMTECH founders supervision and management, were able to build the first Hyperloop pod in Middle-East & Africa in a cooperative work between Cairo University, Ministry of Military Production, and Sypron Solutions.
